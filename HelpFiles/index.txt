Crew Chief is a team radio engineer, originally developed by <b><a href="http://thecrewchief.org/member.php?2-mr_belowski">Mr Belowski</a></b>.

It is a human-like, highly responsive, open source spotter and much, much more including voice recognition, foul language and thousands of driver names.
